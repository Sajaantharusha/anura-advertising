Who We Are

32 years on the printing market

Anura Advertising is a private limited company built on creativity blended with hand-craft and the latest technology. Uniqueness is an oath our team takes on every project.

We serve corporate and individual demand for name boards, vehicle branding and hoardings, and provide every digital printing solution in between. Since 1994, we've been recognised as a leading company in our industry.

Every project comes with zero-hassle handover — from concept to completion — backed by an experienced, safety-first team equipped with the latest technology.
